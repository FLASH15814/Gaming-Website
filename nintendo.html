What are the key features of Nintendo Switch?

The key features of Nintendo Switch include:

Is Nintendo Switch backward compatible?
+

Yes, Nintendo Switch is backward compatible which means it can play games made for the original PS4. The console is also compatible with many popular PlayStation VR games and supports 4K video streaming apps like Netflix, YouTube and Amazon Prime Video.

Can I play multiplayer games on Nintendo Switch?
+

Yes, Nintendo Switch supports all multiplayer features of the original PS4 console. You can enjoy online multiplayer gaming, participate in communities and clubs, stream your gameplay, and more. Popular multiplayer titles like Fortnite, Call of Duty, FIFA, Apex Legends fully support multiplayer on Nintendo Switch.
