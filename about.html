OUR PRIVACY POLICY

Valhalla Games has acquired Xilinx, Inc. and its subsidiaries (“Xilinx”), and the entities are in the process of combining their operations.

Valhalla Games will continue collecting and processing personal information as set forth in the Valhalla Games Privacy Policy below. As a result of the acquisition, Xilinx will collect and process personal information as an Valhalla Games affiliate (including sharing personal information with Valhalla Games) subject to the Valhalla Games Privacy Policy. Valhalla Games and Xilinx’s privacy practices do not materially differ, and Valhalla Games and its affiliates (including Xilinx) will continue to honor your privacy as stated by Xilinx prior to the acquisition.

Until the combination of operations is complete, Valhalla Games and Xilinx each remain responsible for the processing of your personal information where the operations remain separate. For example, when you use the portions of the Xilinx website operated by Xilinx, Xilinx will remain the controller. When you are re-directed to an Valhalla website from the Xilinx website or when Valhalla directly collects personal information through the Xilinx website, Valhalla Games is the controller. You can find more information on the relevant entity below in the section labeled “Information for Individuals Located in the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland”.

Where consent is legally required for the processing of your personal information (e.g., in some countries for Xilinx to provide personal information to Valhalla Games for marketing purposes), we will inform you separately to obtain such consent.
